Why Stephen Curry is the Best Shooter?

At the beginning of Stephen Curry's career, everyone thought he was a nobody. They did not believe he would ever be good and there was many doubts to his basketball skills. But Stephen Curry did not let that stop him. He practiced, and now he is one of the greastes players in the world. He averages 24 points a game, and makes the impossible shots. He makes three pointers look easy, and has won many games with his clutch three pointer. He really is incredible at this game.
